# Who We Are

A premier investment manager, pioneering a unique approach to asset stewardship.

## Our Mission

We believe markets are volatile and client objectives can change over time. Against that backdrop, our goal is to deliver risk-managed, dynamic investment strategies that evolve and improve alongside our clients. Above all, we aim to realize satisfactory returns, while operating within strictly-defined risk limits.

## Our Edge

In our view, there are three reasons we achieve better outcomes than the competition:

1. **An Interdisciplinary Approach** - Traditional portfolio managers delegate deep knowledge to expert analysts, and make portfolio decisions based on a surface-level understanding of a wide number of issues. In contrast, we foster deep, interdisciplinary knowledge at the level of the decision-maker: across asset classes, sectors, and geographies. We believe this approach has been directly responsible for our superior investment outcomes.
2. **Relentless Pursuit of Simplicity** - In our industry, complexity often sounds smart. However, we view investing as a mix of art and science, both of which are defined by simplicity and elegance. Our view is: if one cannot articulate an investment thesis in a sentence or two, one likely doesn't understand the opportunity well enough, and one should not invest.
3. **People First** - Stocks and bonds are issued by companies, which are operated by *people*. Commodities and currencies are bought and sold by monetary authorities and industry, which are institutions comprised of *people*. Therefore, we take an intense interest in understanding how people do business, how people buy products and services, and how people drive value. To our knowledge, few others invest with these factors in mind.

## Our Team

Our founder-led team consists of investment professionals with over a decade of investment management experience across asset classes at top-tier firms globally. We combine that deep market expertise with a client-first approach to deliver our premier asset management services.
